# Bounty Workflow

## Status Model
- **Triage**: Inbound, not approved for claiming.
- **Todo**: Approved and claimable.
- **In Progress**: Claimed and actively worked.
- **In Review**: Submission under review.
- **Done**: Accepted and closed.

## Rules of Engagement
1. **Anti-Parking**: Draft PR required within 48 hours of assignment.
2. **Zero-Leak**: No secrets or private keys in PRs.
3. **Evidence-Based**: No payout without a linked PR and verification.
