# Conxius Monetization Strategy (v1.6.0)

## 💰 Revenue Streams

1. **Protocol Routing Fees**
   - **DeFi Yield**: 0.1% fee on entry via Yield.xyz gateway.
   - **Liquidity Swaps**: 0.3% protocol fee on 1inch/LI.FI aggregated swaps.
2. **Affiliate Commissions**
   - **Parametric Insurance**: 5% commission on Neptune Mutual/InsurAce premiums.
   - **Sovereign Marketplace**: 2-5% share on Travala bookings, Satlantis tickets, and Silent.Link eSIMs.
3. **B2B Integration Fees**
   - **Merchant Gateway**: 25% revenue share on CoinsPaid processing fees.
   - **NTT Bridge**: Technical integration fee capped at $50.

## 🛡️ Sovereignty Alignment
All fees are embedded directly into unsigned transaction payloads, ensuring revenue generation remains strictly non-custodial.
