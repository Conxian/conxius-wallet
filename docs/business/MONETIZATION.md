# Conxius Monetization Strategy (v1.6.0)

## 💰 Revenue Streams

1. **Protocol Routing Fees**

- **Mechanism:** Conxius earns a small routing fee (e.g., 1-5 sats/vByte) for Lightning payments routed through our preferred non-custodial channels or LSPs.
- **Value:** Incentivizes the maintenance of high-uptime Lightning infrastructure.

2. **Cross-Chain / Bridge Convenience Fees**

- **Mechanism:** A 0.1% convenience fee is charged on cross-chain swaps and bridge operations (sBTC, L-BTC, RBTC) performed via the Conxius NTT Transceiver and integrated partners like Boltz, 1inch, and LI.FI.
- **Value:** Revenue from facilitating interoperability and maintaining the NTT infrastructure.

3. **Gas Abstraction Service Fees (The "sBTC-as-Gas" Model)**

- **Mechanism:** When users pay for gas on non-Bitcoin chains (e.g., BOB, ETH) using BTC/sBTC, a service fee is included in the conversion rate. Conxius handles the destination gas payment via its relayer fleet.
- **Value:** Monetizes the convenience of "Bitcoin-only" UX and eliminates the need for users to hold multiple gas tokens.

4. **Yield & Staking Commissions**

- **Mechanism:** Affiliate commissions from yield providers (Yield.xyz, Lido, Aave) or protocol-level rewards for participation in Babylon staking.
- **Value:** Passive revenue from user-opt-in financial services.

5. **Merchant & Marketplace Referrals**

- **Mechanism:** Referral bonuses from Travala (travel), Silent.Link (eSIMs), and other marketplace partners.
- **Value:** Generates revenue from real-world utility without extracting from the user's principal.

6. **B2B SaaS (Conxian Gateway)**

- **Mechanism:** Subscription fees for enterprise users using the Conxian Gateway for treasury management, institutional token launches, and shielded payments.
- **Value:** Recurring revenue from institutional clients.

## 🛡️ Sovereign Ethos

- **No Extraction from Principal:** Fees are only applied to utility/service operations, never to simple storage or P2P transfers.
- **Transparency:** All fees are clearly disclosed in the "Sovereign Handshake" before any transaction is signed.
- **Non-Custodial:** Conxius never takes custody of user funds to generate revenue.
